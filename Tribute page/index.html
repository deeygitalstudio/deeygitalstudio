Dr Orlando Owoh

The King of Kenneries

Here's a timeline of Dr Orlando's life

  • He was born Stephen Oladipupo Olaore Owomoyela in osogbo febuary 14, 1932
  • His father hailed from Ifon Osun, Osun State, while his mother was from Owo
  • Owoh initially entered into the carpentry trade in 1958
  • He was hired by Nigeria's Kola Ogunmola Theatre Group to play drums and sing and Owoh went on to form Dr. Orlando Owoh and his Omimah Band in 1960
  • Owoh had a musical career of over forty years became one of the leading proponents of highlife music.
  • He had over 45 albums to his credit. Orlando Owoh died on 4 November 2008 and was laid to rest at his Agege residence in Lagos, Nigeria.

"With bands such as the Omimah Band and later the Young Kenneries and the African Kenneries International, Owoh remained popular in Nigeria, even as tastes moved to the newer jùjú and fuji styles."

-- Vasco da Gama
